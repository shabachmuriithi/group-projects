Build with us

Join us as we embark on hands-on building activities,
to come up with different machines that make work easier.
These activities foster coperation and teamwork among us
, as we learn different concepts. Through these activities
we have unlocked a higher level of creativity and awareness
of what we can build with simple everyday materials.

Teach it to grandma

In this series we will breakdown and explain seemingly complex concepts in a simple language that grandma would understand and explain to he friends too. This helps us not to just gobble up content but to understand them in the most fundametal way in such a way that we can apply these concepts in any context.

Think critically

Here we will take everyday applicatios that we interact with and understand the numbers behinf the running of these programs. We will imagine different daily life scenarios and calculate what it takes, how much it costs or come up with practical estimations. This will help us to have a deeper undestand of applications as we interact with them everyday.
